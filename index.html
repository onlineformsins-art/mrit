About Us

We are a multidisciplinary research and training hub committed to strengthening health research, professional skills, and community well-being. Our work combines academic guidance, evidence-based training, and community-centered action.

Vision

To become a national hub for multidisciplinary research and training that supports healthier, resilient, and sustainable communities.

Mission

To support impactful research, strengthen capacity, empower communities, and translate evidence into better practice and policy.

Services

Our services are designed to provide practical, ethical, and evidence-based support from planning to implementation.

Research Support

We support researchers from proposal development to final report writing. Practical and ethical research support at every stage.

Services include: proposal writing, methodology guidance, tool development, sample size calculation, ethics approval support, data analysis, thesis guidance, and report writing.

Publication Support

We support researchers in preparing manuscripts for international and local journal submissions. Helping researchers prepare clear, professional, and publication-ready manuscripts.

Services include: manuscript writing, editing, proofreading, journal formatting, reference management, journal selection, plagiarism-check guidance, and reviewer response support.

Training & Workshops

We conduct professional training programs for communities, institutions, students, and professionals. Empowering communities and professionals through practical, evidence-based training.

Services include: public health training, NCD prevention, health promotion, research skills, leadership development, and monitoring and evaluation.

Journal Clubs

We conduct guided journal club sessions for communities, students, researchers, institutions, and professionals. Promoting scientific thinking, research skills, and evidence-based learning.

Services include: critical appraisal, research discussions, statistical interpretation, article reviews, and evidence-based practice discussions.

Community Projects

We design and implement participatory community-based projects that promote health and social development. Creating sustainable community impact through practical and community-centered approaches.

Services include: health awareness programs, school health activities, NCD prevention, youth empowerment, and rural health promotion.

Consultancy Services

We provide professional consultancy support for public health, research, and community-based programs. Supporting evidence-based planning, implementation, and evaluation for meaningful impact.

Services include: public health consultancy, research consultancy, program planning, survey support, community assessments, and baseline/endline evaluations.
